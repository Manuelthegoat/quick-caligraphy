History Of Calligraphy

Calligraphy and where it originated from.

Calligraphy (from Greek: καλλιγραφία) is a visual art related to writing and is the design and execution of lettering with a pen, ink brush, or other writing instrument. Contemporary calligraphic practice can be defined as "the art of giving form to signs in an expressive, harmonious, and skillful manner".
